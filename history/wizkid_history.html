Ayodeji Ibrahim Balogun popularly known as Wizkid is a Nigerian singer and songwriter. who began his music at the age of 11 and has released a collaborative album with the Glorious Five, a 
group he and a couple of his church friends formed in 2009, he was signed into Banky W's E.M.E where he got his fame after releasing Holla at your boy. the lead single from his debut studio
album. In september 2014, he released the self-titled second studio album titled Ayo which contained six singles and he left E.M.E in 2016 after his contract expired and as achieved more
than his former boss. He has grown his music to the stage of Grammy nomination which he recently lost to Angelinea Kidjo
