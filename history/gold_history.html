Adekunle Almoruf Kosoko professionally known as adekunle gold is a nigerian highlife singer, songwriter and graphic designer. He ained widespread attention after releasing the 2015 hit single "Sade", a highlife cover of One Direction's "Story of my life". he was signed into YBNL nation and released his 2015 debut studio album Gold. which debuted at number 7 on the billboard World albums chart. Gold revealed to Nigeria Entertainment Today that prior to signing with YBNL, he designed the label's official logo and completed other designs for Lil Kesh , Viktoh and Olamide
